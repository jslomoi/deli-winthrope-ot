Fees and funding

NDIS

Deli is not a registered NDIS provider but is happy to see self-managed and plan-managed participants.

Private health fund

Your child may be eligible for reimbursement through your private health fund (policy dependent).

Medicare

With an Enhanced Primary Care (EPC) plan from your GP or Paediatrician, you will be eligible to claim a rebate on OT sessions. Please note there will be a gap fee, so there will be out-of-pocket expenses.

Session rates

Get in touch for a current price guide. Deli will be happy to answer any questions about rates and funding options before you commit to anything.
