Paediatric occupational therapy · Melbourne

Helping families navigate big feelings, behaviour, and everyday challenges.

I'm a paediatric occupational therapist specialising in emotional regulation, challenging behaviour and engagement in everyday activities. I take a warm, practical and collaborative approach to help you understand the reasons behind your child's behaviour and implement strategies that fit within your daily life.

I provide neuro-affirming, evidence-based mobile occupational therapy, meeting your child where they're at and supporting them to thrive in their everyday environments.

My work is highly collaborative, with a strong focus on supporting parents and caregivers. Because real change doesn't just happen in sessions. It happens in the moments in between.
